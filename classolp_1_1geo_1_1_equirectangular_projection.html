Detailed Description

Equirectangular (plate carree) projection.

http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Equirectangular_projection

Latitude between -90 and 90 map to Y world axis with range: [0, 0.5] Longitude between -180 and 180 map to X world axis with range: [0, 1] Altitude maps to Z world axis and is not scaled.
