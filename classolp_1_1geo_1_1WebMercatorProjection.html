Detailed Description

The Web Mercator projection.

The world and geographic coordinates are related in the following way:

  • Latitude between -MaximumLatitude() and MaximumLatitude() maps to the Y world axis of the following range: [0, EarthConstants::EquatorialCircumference()].
  • Longitude between -180 and 180 maps to the X world axis of the following range: [0, EarthConstants::EquatorialCircumference()].
  • Altitude maps to the Z world axis and is not scaled.
